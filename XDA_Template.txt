[B][SIZE="7"]FLASHING PROCEDURE[/SIZE][/B]

[SIZE="4"][color="#e34036"][B]CLEAN FLASH :[/B][/SIZE][/COLOR]
[SIZE="4"]Download the Build you want to flash, there are two variants(GAPPS AND VANILLA ).
Reboot to recovery 
Take Backup (Not Necessary)
Wipe System, Data, Cache , Dalvik
Flash the latest build
Reboot[/SIZE]

[SIZE="4"][color="#e34036"][B]DIRTY FLASH :[/B][/SIZE][/COLOR]
[SIZE="4"]Download the latest build 
Reboot to recovery
Flash the latest build and GApps(Not needed if you are flashing GAPPS build)
Wipe Cache
Reboot[/SIZE]
Note : GApps only has to be flashed once whereas Magisk is to be flashed every time you Dirty Flash the ROM.
